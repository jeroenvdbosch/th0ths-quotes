A widget plugin that shows random quotes from your own collection.

== Description ==

*th0th's Quotes* is a widget that shows a random quote from your collection. You can manage your quote collection from administration panel of your Wordpress.

== Installation ==

1. Upload th0ths_quotes folder to your wp-content/plugins/ directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Add widget to your prefered location through the 'Appearance' menu in Wordpress.
1. Manage quotes using "th0th's quotes" menu in administration panel.
